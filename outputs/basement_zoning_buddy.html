The Life Safety Imperative

Why Basement Permits Are Different

Critical Fact: Basement bedrooms without proper egress windows are death traps in fires.

Key safety requirements that MUST be met:

  • Emergency Egress: Minimum 5.7 sq ft opening, max 44" sill height
  • Ceiling Height: 7 feet minimum (6'8" under beams for limited areas)
  • Fire Blocking: Required between floors and at specific intervals
  • Smoke/CO Detectors: Interconnected system throughout home
  • Electrical Safety: GFCI protection in specific areas
