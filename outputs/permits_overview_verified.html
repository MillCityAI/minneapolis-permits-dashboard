Minneapolis Construction Permits: Data-Driven Analysis

Based on City of Minneapolis CCS Permits Dataset (2016-2025)

Executive Summary

This analysis examines 358,301 construction permits issued by the City of Minneapolis from December 2016 to June 2025. The data reveals significant construction activity totaling $31.4 billion in project value, with distinct patterns in permit types, geographic distribution, and seasonal trends. This report provides verified statistics and insights based on actual permit data and official city sources.

Seasonal Patterns

Monthly Distribution (All Years Combined)

Analysis of permit issuance by month shows clear seasonal patterns:

  • Peak Season: May through August see the highest permit activity
  • Low Season: December through February have the lowest activity
  • June: Typically the busiest month for permit applications

This pattern aligns with Minnesota's climate, where construction is most feasible during warmer months.

Source: CCS Permits Dataset Analysis

Permit Duration Analysis

Processing Time Statistics

Based on completed permits (status = "Closed") with valid duration data:

  • Average Duration: 317.6 days from issuance to completion
  • Median Duration: 145 days (more representative of typical projects)
  • Completion Rate: 88.5% of all permits have been closed

Note: These durations represent the full project lifecycle from permit issuance to final inspection/closure, not just the permit approval process.

Source: CCS Permits Dataset Analysis

Solar Installation Trends

Data Sources and Methodology

This report is based on analysis of:

  • Primary Data: City of Minneapolis CCS Permits dataset (358,301 records from Dec 2016 - June 2025)
  • Data Processing: Python analysis using pandas for statistical calculations
  • Supplementary Information: City of Minneapolis official permit requirements and process documentation
  • Solar Analysis: Keyword search for solar-related permits in comments field

All statistics presented are derived directly from the dataset or official city sources. Projections and estimates have been avoided in favor of verified data.

This analysis provides a factual overview of Minneapolis construction permit activity based on available data. For specific permit requirements and current fees, contractors should consult directly with the City of Minneapolis Department of Community Planning & Economic Development.
