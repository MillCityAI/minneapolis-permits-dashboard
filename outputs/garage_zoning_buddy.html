Why Garages Are the Ultimate Zoning Challenge

The Perfect Storm of Complexity

Based on Minneapolis garage construction specifications, contractors must verify:

  1. Setback Requirements: Vary by zone, alley presence, and garage orientation
  2. Lot Coverage: Total impervious surface cannot exceed 35-65% (varies by zone)
  3. Height Limits: 15-24 feet depending on zone and roof type
  4. Size Restrictions: Maximum 676-1,000 sq ft based on lot size
  5. Access Requirements: Alley width, sight triangles, driveway slopes
  6. Design Standards: Must match principal structure in some districts

Result: Even experienced contractors struggle with these overlapping requirements
